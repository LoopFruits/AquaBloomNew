### Description (4,000 characters max)
```
AquaBloom is the hydration app that makes drinking water fun. Grow a virtual plant companion with every sip, earn Bloom Points, and build streaks — think Duolingo, but for water.

Whether you want to boost your energy, improve your skin, or simply drink more water, AquaBloom turns a daily chore into a rewarding game. Track your water intake with a single tap and watch your plant grow from a tiny sprout to a beautiful bloom as you hit your daily goal.

GROW YOUR PLANT COMPANION
• Your plant grows through 5 stages as you drink — from sprout to full bloom
• Hit your daily water goal and watch your plant fully flourish
• Haptic feedback and animations celebrate every growth stage
• A fresh plant to grow every single day

EARN BLOOM POINTS
• Earn points with every sip of water (+5 per drink)
• Bonus points when you reach your daily goal (+50)
• Extra streak bonuses when you're on a 7+ day streak
• Track your daily earnings and lifetime total

TRACK YOUR HYDRATION
• Log water with quick-add buttons or custom amounts
• See your progress with a beautiful animated progress ring
• View your daily intake history with detailed logs
• Build your hydration streak day after day

GENTLE REMINDERS
• Set up to 8 customizable daily reminders
• Friendly messages that motivate you to keep your plant growing
• Adjust your schedule to match your routine
• Never forget to hydrate again

PREMIUM FEATURES (AquaBloom Pro)
• Unlock premium plant species: Sakura, Monstera, Succulent & more
• Advanced weekly and monthly analytics
• Streak Freeze to protect your streak on off days
• Smart reminders that adapt to your routine
• Ad-free experience

SUBSCRIPTION INFO
AquaBloom Pro is available as a monthly ($3.99/month), yearly ($29.99/year), or lifetime ($79.99 one-time) purchase. Monthly and yearly plans include a 7-day free trial. Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage and cancel your subscriptions in your Apple ID account settings.
```
