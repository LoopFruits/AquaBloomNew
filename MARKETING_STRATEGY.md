## Executive Summary

**Target:** Women 18-45, wellness-focused, interested in self-care, skin health, and healthy habits  
**Positioning:** "Hydrate & Glow" - Positioning hydration as self-care, not just health

**Timeline:** Pre-launch (2-4 weeks) → Launch Week → Growth (Months 1-3)

### 3. Paid Ads Strategy ($100-500 Test Budget)

**Platform:** Meta Ads (Instagram + Facebook) - Best for wellness/women-focused apps

**Campaign Structure:**

**Campaign 1: App Install Campaign**
- Objective: App Installs
- Budget: $10/day for 7 days = $70
- Targeting:
  - Women, 18-45
  - Interests: Wellness, Self-Care, Skincare, Health Apps, Fitness
  - Lookalike: Upload waitlist email (if 100+ emails)
- Creative: Carousel ad with 3 slides (app demo, features, Premium benefits)
- CTA: "Install Now"

**Campaign 2: Retargeting (Days 3-7)**
- Objective: Conversions (Premium subscription)
- Budget: $5/day for 5 days = $25
- Targeting: People who installed but haven't subscribed
- Creative: Video testimonial or Premium feature highlight
- CTA: "Start Free Trial"

**Total Test Budget:** $95 (round to $100)

**Success Metrics:**
- Cost Per Install (CPI): Target $1-3
- Install-to-Subscription Rate: Target 2-5%
- If CPI < $2 and conversion rate > 3%, scale budget to $20-50/day

### 3. Conversion Optimization (Paywall A/B Tests)

**Current Paywall:** 7-day free trial, Monthly $3.99, Yearly $29.99

**A/B Test Ideas:**

**Test 1: Pricing**
- Control: $3.99/mo, $29.99/yr (37% savings)
- Variant: $4.99/mo, $34.99/yr (30% savings) ← higher LTV, may hurt conversion

**Test 2: Trial Length**
- Control: 7-day trial
- Variant: 14-day trial ← may increase conversions but delay revenue

**Test 3: Copy Emphasis**
- Control: "Start Your 7-Day Free Trial"
- Variant: "Remove Ads & Unlock Premium Features" ← emphasize pain point

**Test 4: Social Proof**
- Control: Feature list only
- Variant: Add "Join 10,000+ women glowing with AquaBloom Premium"

**How to Test:**
- RevenueCat Experiments (built-in A/B testing)
- Run each test for 1-2 weeks, 1,000+ users minimum
- Winner becomes new control

### Key Metrics & Targets

**Acquisition Metrics:**
- **Cost Per Install (CPI):** $1-3 (organic) | $3-5 (paid ads)
- **Viral Coefficient (K-factor):** 0.3-0.5 (with referral system)
- **App Store Conversion Rate:** 25-35% (visitors → downloads)

**Engagement Metrics:**
- **Day 1 Retention:** 40-50%
- **Day 7 Retention:** 20-30%
- **Day 30 Retention:** 10-15%
- **Daily Active Users (DAU):** 30% of MAU

**Monetization Metrics:**
- **Ad Revenue per DAU:** $0.10-0.30 (eCPM varies by region)
- **Free-to-Paid Conversion:** 2-5% (industry standard)
- **LTV (Lifetime Value):** $15-25 (avg. 6-month subscription)
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** $3-5
- **LTV/CAC Ratio:** 3:1 to 5:1 (healthy = >3:1)

**Break-Even:**
- Occurs around Month 3-4 with organic growth + modest paid ads
- Faster with viral growth (referral system, influencer hits)
