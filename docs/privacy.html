Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 12, 2026

Welcome to AquaBloom ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Water Intake Data: The amount and timing of water you log in the app
  • Goal Settings: Your daily hydration goals and preferences
  • Reminder Preferences: Times and frequency for hydration reminders
  • Premium Subscription Data: Payment information processed through Apple/Google (we do not store payment details)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Features you use, frequency of use, app performance data
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics via PostHog (privacy-focused analytics)

1.3 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and payment processing
  • Google AdMob: Advertising for free tier users (personalized ads can be disabled)
  • PostHog: Privacy-focused analytics (no personal data sold)
  • Apple Health / Google Fit: Optional health data sync (only if you enable it)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide core app functionality (tracking water intake, reminders, gamification)
  • Improve app features and user experience
  • Process subscription payments and manage your Premium account
  • Send you notifications (reminders, streak alerts) if enabled
  • Analyze app usage to identify bugs and performance issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Use your data for purposes unrelated to AquaBloom
  • Share your hydration logs with anyone without your consent

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

Your water intake logs are stored locally on your device. This data is not uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly enable cloud backup (Premium feature).

3.2 Cloud Backup (Premium Only)

If you enable cloud backup, your data is encrypted and stored securely. You can delete your cloud data at any time from Settings.

3.3 Security Measures

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication for Premium accounts
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to data (need-to-know basis)

4. Advertising (Free Tier)

Free users see ads powered by Google AdMob. You can:

  • Opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy → Advertising; Android: Settings → Google → Ads)
  • Upgrade to Premium to remove all ads

Learn more about Google's ad policies: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

5. Health Data Integration

If you enable Apple Health (iOS) or Google Fit (Android) integration:

  • We read/write hydration data only with your explicit permission
  • You can revoke access at any time in your device's health app settings
  • Health data is subject to Apple/Google's privacy policies

6. Children's Privacy

AquaBloom is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@aquabloomapp.com.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Portability: Export your water logs in CSV format
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics, ads, or notifications

To exercise these rights, go to Settings → Privacy or email privacy@aquabloomapp.com.

8. Data Retention

  • Active Users: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data deleted within 30 days (except where required by law)
  • Analytics: Anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for app improvement

9. International Users

AquaBloom is available worldwide. If you are located outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the U.S. By using AquaBloom, you consent to this transfer.

GDPR (European Users): You have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via:

  • In-app notification
  • Email (if you provided one)
  • Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this page

Continued use of AquaBloom after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@aquabloomapp.com
  • Support: support@aquabloomapp.com
  • Website: https://aquabloomapp.com

By using AquaBloom, you agree to this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service.
