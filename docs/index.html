Features

Everything you need to stay hydrated

Built with care for people who want to feel their best — without the complexity.

🪴
Plant Companion System
Your personal plant grows through 5 stages as you hydrate — from empty pot to full bloom. Each stage unlocks at 25%, 50%, 75%, and 100% of your daily goal. It resets every morning, giving you a fresh reason to hydrate every single day.
🌸
Bloom Points
Earn 5 points per sip, 50 points for hitting your goal, and streak bonuses when you've been consistent for 7+ days.
GAMIFIED
🔥
Streak Tracking
Build momentum day by day. Your streak counter motivates you to stay consistent and rewards your dedication.
📊
Daily History Log
See every sip logged with timestamps, a visual timeline, and a daily summary — intake total, average per sip, and % of goal.
🔔
Smart Reminders
8 preset reminders from Morning Rise to Before Bed. Toggle each one individually to match your daily rhythm.
PRO: AI-POWERED
↩️
Undo & Custom Amounts
Made a mistake? Undo the last entry instantly. Need an exact amount? Type any value from 10 to 2,000 ml.
